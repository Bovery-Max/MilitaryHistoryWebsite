Continue Your Military History Journey

Here are recommended resources for those wishing to dive deeper into military history.

Essential Reading

Foundational and insightful books on military history

  • The Art of War by Sun Tzu - Ancient strategy still relevant today
  • On War by Carl von Clausewitz - Philosophical examination of war
  • The Second World War by Winston Churchill - First-hand account of WWII
  • A History of Warfare by John Keegan - Comprehensive overview
  • The Face of Battle by John Keegan - Innovative look at battle experience
  • Liberty or Death by Robert Middlekauff - American Revolution
  • D-Day by Stephen Ambrose - Detailed account of Normandy invasion
  • With the Old Breed by Eugene B. Sledge - Marine's WWII Pacific memoir

Digital Archives and Museums

Online collections and virtual exhibits

  • The National Archives (US) - Military records and documents
  • Imperial War Museums (UK) - Online collections and exhibits
  • Library of Congress Veterans History Project - First-hand accounts
  • Commonwealth War Graves Commission - Memorial databases
  • The Avalon Project (Yale) - Historical documents in law, history, diplomacy
  • HyperWar Foundation - WWII documents and histories
  • Combined Arms Research Library (US Army) - Military studies
  • Naval History and Heritage Command - US Navy resources

Educational Institutions and Programs

Academic resources for serious study

  • West Point Digital History Center - Military Academy resources
  • King's College London War Studies - Leading war studies department
  • University of Leeds Centre for War Studies - Interdisciplinary research
  • Ohio State University Military History - Comprehensive programs
  • Stanford University History Department - Military history courses
  • Cambridge Military Histories - Scholarly book series
  • Journal of Military History - Peer-reviewed academic journal
  • War in History - International journal of military history

Documentaries and Media

Visual and audio resources for learning

  • The World at War (1973) - Comprehensive WWII documentary series
  • Civil War by Ken Burns - Definitive documentary on American Civil War
  • War and Civilization - John Keegan's historical analysis series
  • BBC History Magazine - Regular military history features
  • Military History Quarterly - Magazine with articles and book reviews
  • The Great Courses - Lectures by leading historians
  • Hardcore History by Dan Carlin - Popular history podcast
  • History Hit - Podcast and video content by Dan Snow

Research and Reference Tools

Tools for serious researchers and enthusiasts

  • JSTOR - Academic journal archive (military history collections)
  • Project MUSE - Humanities and social sciences resources
  • Google Books - Searchable database of military history texts
  • WorldCat - Global library catalog for finding books
  • Conflict Text Books - Military manuals and doctrinal publications
  • Osprey Publishing - Detailed illustrated military history books
  • Stackpole Books - Military history reference works
  • University Presses - Specialized military history publishing
